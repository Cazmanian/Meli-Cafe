About Me

Welcome to Meli Café, where Greek tradition meets espresso passion! I grew up surrounded by the aromaas of great food and strong coffee, inspired by mu Greek heritage and my dad--who's been in the restaurant world his whole life. Together, we built this trailer by hand, blending his experience with my love for espresso and golden, honey-soaked loukamades.


Every cup and every bite we serve is made with family pride, craftsmanship, and a touch of Greek hospitality.

What are Loukoumades?

Loukamades are traditional Greek doughnuts. Crispy on the outside, soft inside, and traditionally soaked in honey and sprinked with cinnamon. They're a sweet classic enjoyed for centuries, beste paired with good coffee and good company.

Our Name

Meli means "honey" in Greek -- a nod to both our heritage and the sweetness at the heart of what we do. From golden loukoumades to smooth espresso, Meli Café celebrates simple, authentic flavaors made with care and tradition.
